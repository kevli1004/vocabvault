Getting Started

  • Tap “Start Studying” to begin a session.
  • Review shows only words you missed (after you’ve answered them).
  • You can reset all progress from the Home screen or Mastery view.

FAQ

No words available? Open the app → Reset All Progress → relaunch and try again.

Review repeats a word? It ends automatically after all missed words for the session are reviewed.
