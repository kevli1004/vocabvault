Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 2025

Overview

VocabVault is designed to work offline and stores your study progress on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share personal data with third parties.

Data We Store

  • Study progress (mastery scores, sessions, accuracy) stored locally on your device.
  • App settings and configuration stored locally on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account, email, or contact data.
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking identifiers.
  • No location or precise device data.

Third‑Party Services

None. VocabVault does not integrate external analytics or advertising SDKs.

Game Center (Optional)

If you sign in to Game Center, Apple may process Game Center data per Apple’s policies. This is handled by Apple and not transmitted to us.

Your Choices

  • You can reset all progress from within the app at any time.
  • Deleting the app deletes all locally stored data.
